Five Artists

Their Most Famous Works

Artemisia Gentileschi

She was a painter who used the injustices and oppression she faced in her personal life as inspiration for her work. Her most famous painting is Judith Slaying Holofernes

Antoniazzo Romano

He was a prolific painter throughout his career in Rome. His most famous piece is Annunciation, which hangs in the Church of Santa Maria sopra Minerva

Nicola Salvi

He was an architect and his most famous work is the Trevi Fountain in Rome

Arnaldo Putzu

He was an illustrator and his most famous works are his film posters from the 1960s and 70s

Lorenzo Quinn

He is a sculptor who specializes in human figures. His latest work is Support, which was unveiled at the Venice Biennale
