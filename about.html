History

Ste. Anne de Beaupré School has a very humble and simple beginning. Its owner, founder and Directress, Miss Aurora T. Apuada, had much enthusiasm to begin with. She first envisioned to cater the needs of children with special needs, and with her Special Education (SPED) degree from the University of the Philippines and meager personal funds as capital, she established St. Anne’s Special School in 1974. Housed in a makeshift classroom in a garage in #7 Cattleya St. Mindanao Avenue Subdivision, in Quezon City, the school started its operations with only four students for the school year 1974-1975. With the coming years, substantial increase in enrollment came by, so St. Anne’s School had to transfer to a bigger and better place. Due to its efficient operations, constant improvement of facilities and the inspiring transformation of its student-products, the school was recognized shortly in 1980 – the first private special school DECS-NCR to be granted full recognition.

Seeing the need to put up a school for the purpose of mainstreaming the special children and by insistent request of parents who believe so much in her ability to make a difference even for the regular children, the founder-directress established Ste. Anne de Beaupré School, her regular school in 1987. Starting with the pilot Pre-School Level, the other levels soon followed one after the other, year after year, with all courses duly recognized by the DECS. Ste. Anne de Beaupré School is very proud to have its first batch of High School graduates in March, 1996. From then on, every year sees a new batch of proud and able high school graduates – Ste. Anne graduates molded to possess the necessary wisdom, training, skills and knowledge needed for the more arduous task of tertiary education, and life in general. The graduates have been admitted to prestigious schools and universities. Annean graduates have become professionals in different fields, such as in medicine, law, architecture, engineering, IT, business and education. Now on its 36th year, expect the school to once again deliver awesome activities, its top-notch educational system, and its trademark strict but reasonable adherence to values formation for all students.
